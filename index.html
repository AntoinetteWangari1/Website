    GIS Developer

    I am working on a project on Wetland Mapping and Monitoring of Yala Swamp. Yala Swamp is a critical ecological region in Western Kenya, serving as the largest freshwater wetland in the country. It provides numerous ecosystem services, including water filtration, flood control, and habitat for a diverse range of flora and fauna. The swamp supports local communities through agriculture and fishing, making it vital for both environmental and socio-economic reasons. Therefore, the need for the wetland to be monitored and conserved.
